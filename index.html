About Lokmanya Tilak College of Arts, Commerce and Science, Sevli

Established in 2009, Lokmanya Tilak College of Arts, Commerce and Science, Sevli is a prominent institution under the management of Jijae Prathishthan Sevli. The college was founded to make higher education accessible and affordable for students from rural and semi-urban areas.

Affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad, the college offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs including B.A., B.Com., B.Sc., B.C.A., and M.Lib. Its academic offerings are designed to blend traditional knowledge with modern career-oriented learning.

With a focus on all-round student development, the college provides well-equipped laboratories, a digital library, ICT-enabled classrooms, and a strong mentorship ecosystem led by experienced faculty members.

Lokmanya Tilak College is recognized for its disciplined environment, cultural activities, and community-oriented programs. It continues to play a vital role in shaping future leaders and responsible citizens through quality education.

Mission and Vision Statement

Lokmanya Tilak College of Arts, Commerce and Science Strives to Foster Knowledge, Ethics, and Excellence.

Our Mission

At Lokmanya Tilak College, our mission is to provide quality education that integrates academic excellence with cultural, social, and moral values. We aim to nurture responsible citizens through holistic development and a learner-centric environment.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading educational institution that inspires innovation, fosters inclusivity, and contributes to nation-building by empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values for a better society.

LIFE AT LT

Campus Life

Lokmanya Tilak College of Arts, Commerce and Science nurtures the dreams of every student by shaping them into well-rounded individuals. The college features a modern campus with Wi-Fi enabled infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, spacious seminar halls, a strong placement cell, and a hygienic in-house canteen. The library, spread across two floors, offers both physical and digital resources to enhance learning. The institution encourages student engagement in sports and extracurricular activities, promoting creativity and personal growth.

Hostel

Separate hostels for boys and girls are located within walking distance from the campus, ensuring convenience and safety. The hostels are designed to be an extension of the home, fostering an environment conducive to academic focus and holistic development. The institution's values and discipline are maintained throughout the hostel system, encouraging a respectful and responsible student community.
