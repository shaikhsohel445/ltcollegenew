BCA Department Profile

The Department of Computer Science & Design (CSD) is established in the year 2021. The current intake of the UG program is 60. The main focus of the department is to produce graduates with expertise in Computer Science and Design domain. The college is accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). UG Program :

Bachelor of Computer Applications (B.C.A) is a 3-year undergraduate degree program offered under the Department of Computer Applications. The department has an approved intake of 60 students with a provision for 10% lateral entry in the second year for eligible diploma holders.

Highlights Of The Department:

  • Experienced and dedicated faculty with strong academic and industry backgrounds
  • Well-equipped computer laboratories with the latest hardware and software
  • Smart classrooms and high-speed internet facilities
  • Focus on student development through seminars, workshops, and coding contests
  • Guest lectures and training sessions from industry professionals
  • Exposure to trending technologies such as AI, IoT, and Data Analytics

Career Opportunities :

The BCA program opens a wide range of career paths in IT and related industries. Graduates can pursue roles such as:

  • Software Developer / Programmer
  • Web Developer / Frontend Engineer
  • Database Administrator
  • System Analyst
  • Mobile App Developer
  • UI/UX Designer
  • Opportunities in Animation, Gaming, Augmented/Virtual Reality, etc.

Students may also choose to pursue higher studies like MCA, M.Sc (CS), or enter competitive exams for government and research opportunities.

Department Overview

The BCA program is designed to provide a solid foundation in computing principles, programming, databases, and software engineering with industry relevance.

Vision

To prepare skilled and distinct professionals in Computer Applications with ethical values and research capabilities.

Mission

  • Build a strong academic foundation in computing concepts.
  • Encourage innovation and research in the software domain.
  • Foster social responsibility and lifelong learning.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • PEO1: Develop programming and analytical skills.
  • PEO2: Enable higher studies and industry readiness.
  • PEO3: Promote ethical and socially responsible professionals.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • PSO1: Apply programming concepts in real-world software development.
  • PSO2: Use modern computing tools and techniques.

Program Outcomes (POs)

  • PO1: Knowledge of computing fundamentals.
  • PO2: Analyze problems and design solutions.
  • PO3: Effective use of modern IT tools.
  • PO4: Awareness of societal and ethical responsibilities.
