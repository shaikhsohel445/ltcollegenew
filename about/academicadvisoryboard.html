Academic Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Arts (B.A.): 3-year undergraduate degree
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.): 3-year undergraduate degree
  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.): 3-year undergraduate degree
  • Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA): 3-year program in Computer and Management
  • Master of Library & Information Science (M.Lib.): 2-year postgraduate degree

Academic Policies & Requirements

  • Semester system implemented from the academic year 2009â€“2010 for all faculties.
  • In the Arts faculty, students must choose three optional subjects from different groups in the first year, which remain unchanged till the final year.
  • In the final (third) year, students must select one optional subject as their Honors specialization.
  • Compulsory Computer Training: All students who do not have Computer Science as a subject must complete a 100-hour certified computer literacy training within the 3 years.
  • Compulsory Environmental Studies: All B.A., B.Com., and B.Sc. students must complete a 100-mark Environmental Studies course within their degree period. Failure to complete it will result in disqualification from the final-year university examination.
