Dr. Vijay Sharma
PRINCIPAL

Hearty congratulations on choosing Lokmanya Tilak College of Arts, Commerce and Science, Sevli — an institution dedicated to academic excellence, rural empowerment, and holistic student development.

I warmly welcome you all to this esteemed center of learning where education is not just about acquiring degrees, but about shaping character, strengthening ethics, and building confident, responsible citizens.

At Lokmanya Tilak College, we believe in nurturing both academic and life skills through a well-structured curriculum, co-curricular engagements, and dedicated mentorship by our experienced faculty.

Our institution is affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad and is working steadily toward future accreditations and academic benchmarks like NAAC, while aligning with university reforms and academic excellence.

The college provides a vibrant learning atmosphere where students actively participate in NSS, cultural activities, skill-based clubs, and community initiatives. These platforms offer students opportunities to discover their potential beyond the classroom and foster leadership, teamwork, and creativity.

Our management, under the guidance of Jijae Prathishthan Sevli, comprises dedicated educators and community leaders who are committed to uplifting the region through quality education. The college continues to move forward with a clear mission — “Education for Empowerment.”

I invite you to be part of this academic journey and take full advantage of the opportunities and values that Lokmanya Tilak College offers. Let us work together in building a brighter and meaningful future for all.
