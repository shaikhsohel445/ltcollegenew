About Lokmanya Tilak College of Science and Commerce, Sevli

Established in 2009, Lokmanya Tilak College of Arts, Science and Commerce, Sevli is a premier institution in Jalna district, run by Jijae Prathishthan Sevli. The college was founded with a mission to empower rural students through quality and affordable higher education.

The college is affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad and offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs including B.A., B.Com., B.Sc., B.C.A., and M.Lib. The academic curriculum is designed to balance foundational knowledge with practical and career-oriented skills.

Over the years, Lokmanya Tilak College has built a reputation for academic discipline, student-centric learning, and community development. It serves as an educational hub for students across the region, especially from rural and semi-urban backgrounds.

The institution features well-equipped laboratories, a digital library, ICT-enabled classrooms, and a dedicated faculty focused on student success. Special attention is given to moral values, soft skills, and co-curricular activities that help shape well-rounded individuals.

With consistent growth and a commitment to quality, Lokmanya Tilak College continues to stand as a symbol of educational empowerment in Maharashtra.
