Welcome To Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

Objective (As Per NAAC)

The primary aim of IQAC is:

  • To develop a system for conscious, consistent, and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution.
  • To promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.

Roles and Responsibilities

This section outlines the specific roles and responsibilities of the Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) within the institution. Key responsibilities include:

  • Developing quality benchmarks.
  • Facilitating the creation of a learner-centric environment.
  • Collecting and analyzing feedback from stakeholders.
  • Organizing workshops and seminars on quality-related themes.

Composition

The IQAC is composed of a diverse group of members representing various stakeholders of the institution to ensure comprehensive quality assurance. The typical composition includes:

  • Head of the Institution (Chairperson)
  • Senior administrative functionaries
  • Teachers
  • Members from the management
  • Local society, students, and alumni
